ChatGPT workflow (8 steps)
  1. Upload the approved examples — Upload one or more approved 1:1 masters so ChatGPT can see the MedVirtual baseline.
  2. Tell ChatGPT to analyze only — Ask for hierarchy, contrast, and role clarity notes — do not invent claims.
  3. Request original concepts — Ask for 15–20 original concepts in the approved direct-response style. Explicitly say NO PINK and do not copy competitors.
  4. Select one direction — Pick the strongest concepts for design or AI plate testing.
  5. Confirm exact copy — Lock headline, benefits, offer, and CTA before final art.
  6. Generate or brief the designer — AI-generated ads may be used for testing. Designers may also build from the brief directly.
  7. Check every word — Verify spelling, price, services, Spanish, badges, logos, hands, and accidental pink.
  8. Build required aspect ratios — Rebuild approved concepts for 1:1, 4:5, 9:16, and 1.91:1 — not a crop.
